### MY2025 - Production start 2024

The Audi Q6 e-tron had its world premiere on March 18, 2024. Production takes place at the Ingolstadt plant on the new [PPE platform](../../technology/bev-platforms/ppe/).

The first customers received their cars in the summer of 2024.

### MY2025 -> MY2026 (2025)

Audi announced comprehensive updates for the Q6 e-tron for the 2026 model year. Orders start from calendar week 48/49 2025. [Source](https://www.audi-mediacenter.com/en/press-releases/updates-for-five-audi-model-series-more-emotion-comfort-and-features-in-store-16916)

#### Driving dynamics

- Audi drive select assistant available - automatically adjusts driving modes to match driving style
- Improved regenerative braking - vehicles can now decelerate to a standstill without transitioning to conventional friction brakes, improving efficiency and range through additional kinetic energy recovery

#### Driver assistance

- Advanced adaptive cruise assist with assisted lane changes on highways via turn signal activation
- Predictive assistance recognizing stop signs, right-of-way, and speed limits via data fusion and front camera
- Swarm data detection of road irregularities shared with other Audi vehicles
- Park assist pro expansion including reverse assist for final 50 meters, maneuver assist, and trained parking (storing and executing five 200-meter maneuvers)
- Garage parking via smartphone without needing to drive past the space first

#### Audi assistant

- Self-learning voice assistant expanded with artificial intelligence
- ChatGPT integration for intuitive cross-functional operation
- Access to vehicle logbook information for detailed questions
- Connected in-car office accessing calendars and emails
- Voice control for driver assist systems and behavioral pattern recognition
- Automatic routine adoption

#### Dashcam (optional)

- Integrated into interior mirror base
- 4K video with HDR and light-sensitive sensor
- Ring buffer recording capturing 30 seconds before and after incidents
- Local SD card storage only
- Automatic triggering via hazard lights or emergency braking

#### Experience Worlds

- Activating, Relaxing, and Harmonizing mood scenarios
- Utilizes interior lighting, sound, massage functions, and climate control
- Duration: 10-20 minutes with dynamic adaptation to vehicle movements
- Power Nap mode during charging breaks

#### Gaming and entertainment

- Bluetooth controller support for games like Asphalt Legends and Queen Rock Tour
- Wireless Bluetooth headphone connectivity
- Active privacy mode on MMI passenger display
- Access to Audi Application Store
