The Audi Q6 e-tron uses the Digital Stage introduced with the Premium Platform Electric. Its curved panoramic display combines an 11.9-inch Audi virtual cockpit with a 14.5-inch MMI touch display. Both use OLED technology and are angled towards the driver.

{{< sitefiguresized thumb="https://media.evkx.net/ehga/models/q6-e-tron/technology/uiandoperations/mmi/mmi_3_st.webp" width="3000" height="2077" title="Audi Q6 e-tron Digital Stage with Audi virtual cockpit, MMI touch display and optional passenger display" >}}

The infotainment system is based on Android Automotive OS and supports over-the-air updates. A built-in app store provides market-dependent access to services such as music, video, navigation, parking and charging apps without requiring a connected smartphone.

## Front-passenger display

An optional 10.9-inch MMI front-passenger display extends the Digital Stage. Active Privacy Mode limits the driver's view of distracting content while the car is moving, allowing the passenger to stream video or help with navigation.

{{< sitefiguresized thumb="https://media.evkx.net/ehga/models/q6-e-tron/technology/uiandoperations/mmi/passengerscreen_1.webp" width="3000" height="2249" title="Audi Q6 e-tron MMI front-passenger display" >}}

The Audi assistant provides natural-language voice control for vehicle and infotainment functions. Depending on equipment, an augmented-reality head-up display supplements the Digital Stage with navigation and driver-assistance information projected into the driver's field of view.
