> **Historical equipment snapshot:** This overview reflects a European launch-market configuration from 2024. Package names, option codes, dependencies and standard equipment vary by country and model year. Check the current national configurator before ordering.

## Technology packages

### Tech

The launch-market Tech package grouped convenience and lighting features such as upgraded LED lights, high-beam assist, a 360-degree camera, park assist plus, convenience key and digital key.

### Tech plus

Tech plus could add Matrix LED headlights, selectable digital light signatures, the MMI passenger display, adaptive cruise assist plus, powered front seats with driver memory, e-tron sport sound and expanded side-assist and occupant-protection functions.

### Tech pro

The highest package could add digital OLED rear lights, a head-up display, adaptive air suspension, soft-close doors, an electrically adjustable steering column and a garage-door opener. Q6 and SQ6 package contents were not identical.

## Exterior choices

The 2024 range offered the standard advanced exterior, S line and SQ6-specific styling. Options included black exterior packages, several roof-rail finishes, a panoramic roof, virtual exterior mirrors and multiple wheel designs.

Wheel availability depended on body style and equipment line. The European launch range covered sizes from **18 to 22 inches**, although not every size was available on every variant. Larger wheels generally reduce range and can make the ride firmer.

## Interior and comfort

The main decisions included standard or sport seats, upholstery and interior design packages, seat heating and ventilation, climate-control upgrades, steering-wheel design, ambient lighting and Bang & Olufsen audio. Some comfort features required a specific seat or technology package.

## Driver assistance and safety

Assistance packages combined functions such as adaptive cruise assist, lane guidance, side assist, exit warning, rear cross-traffic assist, park assist and surround-view cameras. Availability and whether a function was standard or optional differed by market and trim.

## Before ordering

- Verify the current package contents and dependencies in the national configurator.
- Compare homologated range for the selected body style and wheel size.
- Confirm whether app-based or on-demand functions require an Audi connect subscription.
- Check towing equipment compatibility if a tow bar and trailer assistant are required.
