The closed, inverted Singleframe is integrated into a dark mask that also contains the headlights and sensors. Depending on the design line and market, contrasting or black exterior packages change the finish of the Singleframe surround, window trim, roof rails, mirror housings and badges.

Combinations and package names vary by variant, country and model year.
