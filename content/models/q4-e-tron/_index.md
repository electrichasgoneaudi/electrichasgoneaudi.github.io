The **Audi Q4 e-tron** is Audi's compact electric SUV, available with a conventional SUV roofline or as the **Q4 Sportback e-tron**. Audi announced a major update in April 2026, with European ordering from May and first deliveries planned for summer 2026. This site treats the updated car as **model year 2027**.

The update brings more efficient drivetrains, up to **592 km WLTP range**, faster DC charging, bidirectional charging, revised exterior details, and a completely redesigned digital interior.

## Variants and drivetrain

The MY2027 range uses two battery sizes and four drivetrain levels, with market-specific combinations:

- **63 kWh gross / 59 kWh usable** battery
- **82 kWh gross / 77 kWh usable** battery
- Rear-wheel drive with 150 or 210 kW
- Quattro all-wheel drive with 220 or 250 kW

The Q4 Sportback e-tron performance reaches up to **592 km WLTP**. Quattro performance versions charge at up to **185 kW**, with a stated 10–80% charging time of around **27 minutes**. See [variants](variants/) and [drivetrain](drivetrain/) for details.

## Bidirectional charging

The updated Q4 e-tron is the first Audi designed for bidirectional charging. **Vehicle-to-Load (V2L)** can power external equipment through a 230 V socket in the luggage compartment or an optional charging-port adapter. **Vehicle-to-Home (V2H)** is initially offered with compatible home-energy systems in Germany, Austria, and Switzerland.

Plug & Charge is standard for automatic authentication at compatible public chargers. Read more in the [charging section](technology/chargingsystem/).

## Exterior

The facelift introduces a body-color Singleframe, revised spoilers and diffuser, new paint colors, and additional wheel designs. Digital LED daytime-running-light signatures and second-generation digital OLED rear lights extend the lighting options.

## Redesigned interior

The MY2027 dashboard uses an **11.9-inch instrument display** and a **12.8-inch MMI touchscreen**. An optional **12-inch passenger display** allows the front passenger to use entertainment and navigation functions without distracting the driver. An augmented-reality head-up display remains available.

The new center console includes two cooled 15 W inductive charging trays. Four USB-C ports are standard, with two more optional, and approximately 25 liters of storage is distributed through the cabin.

## Technology and equipment

The updated infotainment system integrates the Audi assistant with ChatGPT. Available assistance systems include adaptive driving assistant plus, highway lane-change assist, trained parking, reverse assist, park assist plus, and surround-view cameras. Availability varies by market and equipment package.
