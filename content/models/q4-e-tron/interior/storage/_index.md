> **MY2027 and later:** The redesigned center console includes two cooled 15 W inductive phone-charging trays. Total distributed cabin storage is approximately 25 liters.

## Front cup holders

The center console combines cup holders with open storage for small items and dedicated phone areas. Depending on equipment, the phones can be charged wirelessly while active cooling helps control charging temperature.

## Rear cup holders

The folding center armrest provides cup holders for rear passengers.

## Door compartments

Large storage compartments in all four doors provide space for bottles and everyday items.

## Seat storage

Pockets on the front-seat backrests give rear passengers additional storage for smaller items.
