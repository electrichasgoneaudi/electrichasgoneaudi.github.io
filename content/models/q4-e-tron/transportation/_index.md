## Towing capacity

Audi Q4 e-tron can drag trailers up to 1200kg with Audi Q4 e-tron 45/50 Quattro and 1000kg Audi Q4 e-tron 40.
The towing hitch has option id **1M6**.  An optional towing hitch prepartion has option id **1D7**
