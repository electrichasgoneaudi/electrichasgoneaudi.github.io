The curved OLED MMI panoramic display combines an 11.9-inch Audi virtual cockpit with a 14.5-inch MMI touch display. The infotainment system uses Android Automotive OS and supports over-the-air updates and market-dependent apps through an integrated store.

## Passenger screen

An optional 10.9-inch MMI front-passenger display extends the Digital Stage. Active Privacy Mode limits the driver's view of distracting content while the car is moving.
