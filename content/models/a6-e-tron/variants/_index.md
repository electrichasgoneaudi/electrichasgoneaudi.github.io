The European A6 e-tron range combines four drivetrains with two body styles. Availability and equipment can vary by market.

| Variant | Drive | Battery gross/net | Power | 0–100 km/h | WLTP range |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| A6 Sportback e-tron | Rear-wheel drive | 83/75.8 kWh | 210 kW, 240 kW with Launch Control | 6.0 s | Up to 627 km |
| A6 Avant e-tron | Rear-wheel drive | 83/75.8 kWh | 210 kW, 240 kW with Launch Control | 6.0 s | Up to 598 km |
| A6 Sportback e-tron performance | Rear-wheel drive | 100/94.9 kWh | 270 kW, 280 kW with Launch Control | 5.4 s | Up to 756 km |
| A6 Avant e-tron performance | Rear-wheel drive | 100/94.9 kWh | 270 kW, 280 kW with Launch Control | 5.4 s | Up to 720 km |
| A6 Sportback e-tron quattro | All-wheel drive | 100/94.9 kWh | 315 kW, 340 kW with Launch Control | 4.5 s | Up to 716 km |
| A6 Avant e-tron quattro | All-wheel drive | 100/94.9 kWh | 315 kW, 340 kW with Launch Control | 4.5 s | Up to 685 km |
| S6 Sportback e-tron | All-wheel drive | 100/94.9 kWh | 370 kW, 405 kW with Launch Control | 3.9 s | Up to 675 km |
| S6 Avant e-tron | All-wheel drive | 100/94.9 kWh | 370 kW, 405 kW with Launch Control | 3.9 s | Up to 647 km |

The 83 kWh versions charge at up to **225 kW DC**. Versions with the 100 kWh battery support up to **270 kW DC**. Under ideal conditions, both battery sizes can charge from 10 to 80 percent in approximately 21 minutes.

The A6 e-tron, performance and quattro variants have a top speed of **210 km/h**. The S6 e-tron raises the limit to **240 km/h**.
