The Audi Q5 e-tron is built on Volkswagen Group's **MEB platform**, the same architecture underpinning the Audi Q4 e-tron, Skoda Enyaq, and Volkswagen ID.4/ID.6.

{{< evkxfiguresized thumb="models/audi/q5_e-tron/q5_e-tron_50_quattro/exterior_33cdd_st.webp" width="1920" height="1080" title="Audi Q5 e-tron" >}}

## Electric motors

Two powertrain configurations are available:

| Variant | Power | Torque | Drive | 0–100 km/h |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Q5 e-tron 40 | 150 kW (204 hp) | 310 Nm | Rear-wheel drive | 9.3 s |
| Q5 e-tron 50 quattro | 225 kW (306 hp) | 460 Nm | All-wheel drive | 6.7 s |

Both variants have a top speed limited to **160 km/h**.

## Battery

Both variants use the same battery pack:

| Specification | Value |
|---|---|
| Gross capacity | 83.4 kWh |
| Net capacity | 76.6 kWh |
| Chemistry | NMC |

## Range

| Variant | CLTC range | Consumption |
|---|---|---|
| Q5 e-tron 40 | 560 km | 15.9 kWh/100 km |
| Q5 e-tron 50 quattro | 550 km | 16.2 kWh/100 km |

## Charging

The charge port is located on the **left rear side** of the vehicle.

{{< evkxfiguresized thumb="models/audi/q5_e-tron/q5_e-tron_50_quattro/chargport_08ba5_st.webp" width="1920" height="1080" title="Audi Q5 e-tron charge port" >}}

## Suspension

The Q5 e-tron uses a proven MEB suspension setup:

- **Front**: MacPherson strut with monotube dampers
- **Rear**: Multi-link with monotube dampers
- Ground clearance: **180 mm**
- Optional adaptive damping (electronically adjustable front and rear)

## Towing

- Max trailer weight (braked): **1,200 kg**
- Max trailer weight (unbraked): **750 kg**
- Max tow ball weight: **75 kg**
