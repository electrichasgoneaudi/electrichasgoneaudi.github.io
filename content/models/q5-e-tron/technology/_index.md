## Driver assistance systems

The Q5 e-tron is equipped with a comprehensive suite of driver assistance features:

- Adaptive cruise control
- Lane keeping assist and lane departure warning
- Forward collision warning with automatic emergency braking
- Rear cross traffic alert
- Blind spot monitoring
- Reversing camera
- Parking sensors (front and rear)
- Hill start assist
- Traffic sign recognition

## Sensors

The Q5 e-tron uses a multi-sensor approach for driver assistance:

- **Ultrasonic sensors** — covering all corners for close-range parking assistance
- **Cameras** — front, rear, and corner-mounted cameras for surround awareness
- **Radar** — forward-facing radar for adaptive cruise control and emergency braking
