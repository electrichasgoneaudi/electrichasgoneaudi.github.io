All current e-tron GT variants use quattro all-wheel drive, a 105 kWh gross / 97 kWh usable battery and DC charging at up to 320 kW. Maximum power figures below include Launch Control where applicable.

## Audi e-tron GT quattro

- Maximum output: **430 kW** with Launch Control (**370 kW** otherwise)
- 0–100 km/h: **4.0 seconds**
- Maximum range: **622 km WLTP**

This entry model joined the updated range in 2025 and has the longest quoted range in the family.

## Audi RS e-tron GT performance

The RS e-tron GT performance is the most powerful series-production Audi announced for this model generation.

## First-generation variants

The original Audi e-tron GT and RS e-tron GT premiered in February 2021 and were replaced by the updated family in 2024.
