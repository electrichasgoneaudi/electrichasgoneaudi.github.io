Audi follows a multi-supplier strategy for high-voltage batteries. Cell supplier, chemistry, module design, and final battery assembly can vary by model, production plant, market, and model year.

Supplier information should therefore be checked for the specific vehicle rather than applied to the entire Audi electric range. The model pages describe the battery capacity and chemistry where confirmed; generated specifications contain the detailed data available for each variant.
