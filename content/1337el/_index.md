> **Historical sale listing:** This page was published in 2025. Availability, mileage, warranty, and pricing have not been updated and must not be interpreted as a current offer for sale.

This 2022 Audi e-tron 55 S line was configured with an unusually extensive equipment list. It was finished in Daytona grey and supplied with 22-inch summer wheels and black 20-inch winter wheels. At the time of the listing, the car had covered approximately 52,000 km and had mainly been used for weekend trips between Oslo and Trysil.

## Highlights at the time of publication

- Individual contour seats with 18-way adjustment, ventilation, and massage
- Night-vision assistant
- Head-up display and Audi virtual cockpit plus
- Matrix LED headlights
- Bang & Olufsen premium sound system
- Four-zone climate control
- Panoramic glass roof
- Adaptive cruise and the Tour and City assistance packages
- Surround-view cameras and park assist
- Adaptive air suspension and all-wheel steering
- 22-inch summer wheels and 20-inch winter wheels

The owner reported normally storing the battery at approximately 60% state of charge, using AC charging for most charging sessions, and using DC fast charging only on longer holiday journeys.
