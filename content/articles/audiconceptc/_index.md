## Audi Concept C: A New Era of Clarity, Emotion, and Electric Performance

The **:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} Concept C** is the first tangible expression of Audi’s new design philosophy. Road-approved and fully electric, the two-seat roadster embodies four defining principles that will shape the brand’s future: **clarity, emotional appeal, technical excellence, and intelligent design**. More than a show car, the Concept C offers a realistic preview of how these values will soon be experienced from behind the wheel.

The concept made its public debut in Milan in early September, followed shortly by its appearance at a dedicated stand at the **:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}**. From the outset, the Audi Concept C impressed with its striking balance of precision and emotion—signaling a clear evolution of Audi’s design language.

## A Vision of a Future Electric Sports Car

As a street-legal concept vehicle, the Audi Concept C previews a future production model: an all-electric sports car that places **design, performance, and emotional driving** at its core. The roadster concept blends the exhilaration of open-top driving with the elegance and cohesion of a closed body, thanks to an innovative hardtop solution.

An electrically retractable hardtop—used on an Audi roadster for the first time—consists of two roof elements that preserve the vehicle’s monolithic silhouette when closed, while enabling a true open-air experience when retracted.

## Exterior Design: Athletic Minimalism and a New Brand Face

The Audi Concept C introduces a bold and unmistakable identity defined by **athletic minimalism**. Its proportions are shaped by a central battery layout, resulting in a long wheelbase, a rearward-set cabin, and a confident, planted stance.

### The Vertical Frame

At the front, the newly interpreted **vertical frame** becomes the central design element from which the entire vehicle volume develops. Inspired by the historic **:contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}** and the third-generation Audi A6 (2004), the upright form conveys presence and continuity while integrating modern sensor technology and prominently framing the Audi rings.

A strong shoulder line defines the body’s volume, while clean surfaces and horizontal rear slats emphasize the Concept C’s sporty character.

### A Distinctive Light Signature

The Concept C debuts a new lighting identity featuring **four horizontally arranged elements** in both the headlights and rear lights. This signature will define Audi’s visual presence by day and night, ensuring immediate recognition on the road.

The exterior color, *Titanium*, reflects warm technical elegance—drawing inspiration from the metal’s precision, strength, and lightness.

## Interior: Where Technology Meets Emotion


::contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}


Inside, the Audi Concept C creates a carefully balanced environment that places the driver at the center without excluding the passenger. Strong architectural surfaces and clear geometric forms define the cabin, while refined materials elevate the sensory experience.

Physical controls machined from anodized aluminum deliver a high level of mechanical quality, reinforced by the unmistakable **“Audi click.”** The steering wheel serves as the core interaction point, featuring precision-crafted haptic elements and Audi rings made from real metal.

The color palette follows a titanium-inspired, tone-on-tone approach. Natural materials and indirect ambient lighting with warm hues highlight textures and surfaces, creating a calm yet sophisticated atmosphere.

## Intelligent Technology, Subtly Integrated

Technology in the Audi Concept C is designed to **support, not dominate**. Digital elements are always close at hand but remain visually restrained.

A foldable **10.4-inch center display** stays hidden until activated, preserving an uncluttered interior. When deployed, it presents information intuitively and contextually—tailored to the driving situation. Combined with haptic controls on the steering wheel and center console, the operating concept delivers clarity, precision, and ease of use.

## Continuing Audi’s Design Legacy

Bold innovation paired with uncompromising design has long been part of Audi’s DNA. Iconic models such as the Audi TT, **:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}**, and **:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}** exemplify this philosophy.

The Audi Concept C confidently continues this lineage—reinterpreting it for the electric era and marking a defining step forward for the four rings.

## A Trailblazer for Audi’s Future

The Audi Concept C represents more than a single vehicle study. It marks the beginning of a **new design era** focused on clarity, technical precision, intelligence, and emotion. As a preview of a future production model, it will influence Audi’s design direction well beyond the sports car segment—shaping how the brand presents itself in an all-electric future.
