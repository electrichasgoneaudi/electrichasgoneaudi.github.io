Paint trims

Basic paint

On the basic trim the bumpers and wheel arches is not painted

Contrast paint

You can choose to get bumpers and wheel arches in contrast paint. This is option 2K5 The contrast color is manhatten gray on all options except glacier white and florett silver. On those it is typhoon gray.

Full body paint

You can also choose full body paint so bumpers and wheel archers are painted in body color. This has option VJ1 and is standard on S-line and S exterior trim.
