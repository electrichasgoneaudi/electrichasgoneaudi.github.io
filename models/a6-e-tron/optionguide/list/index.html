Options Guide

Audi A6 e-tron equipment package overview

An overview of the main Audi A6 e-tron technology, comfort, exterior and chassis option groups.

Last updated Jul 15, 2026
Audi A6 e-tron Variants Exterior Specifications Drivetrain Interior Technology Model Year changes Options Guide

Audi combines many A6 e-tron options into market-specific packages. The hierarchy below describes the common European launch structure, but names and contents can change by country, equipment line and model year.

Technology packages

Tech

Typical features include upgraded LED lighting, rear light upgrades, high-beam assist, a 360-degree camera, park assist plus, convenience key and digital key.

Tech plus

Tech plus builds on Tech and can add Matrix LED headlights, selectable digital light signatures, the MMI passenger display, adaptive cruise assist plus, powered front seats with driver memory, expanded occupant protection and additional side-assist functions.

Tech pro

The highest technology package can add digital OLED rear lights, adaptive air suspension on applicable A6 variants, soft-close doors, an electrically adjustable steering column and a garage-door opener. The exact package differs between A6 and S6 equipment lines.

Comfort and interior

Important choices include standard, sport or sport seats plus; upholstery and interior packages; seat heating, ventilation or massage; four-zone climate control; the panoramic glass roof; and the Bang & Olufsen sound systems. Some combinations require a specific technology or interior package.

Exterior and chassis

Exterior choices include paint finish, wheel design, S line or S-specific styling, black exterior packages, brake-caliper finish and virtual exterior mirrors. Wheel dimensions and chassis options affect comfort, efficiency and homologated range.

The A6 e-tron is available with steel-spring suspension and, depending on configuration, adaptive air suspension. Progressive steering and all-wheel steering availability depends on the selected drivetrain and market.

Before ordering

  • Verify package contents in the current national configurator.
  • Check dependencies between technology, interior and assistance packages.
  • Compare range figures for the selected wheel size.
  • Confirm whether optional functions require an Audi connect subscription or later activation.
