Audi Q8 e-tron garage door opener

You can order Audi Q8 e-tron with a programmable garage door opener.

Last updated Jul 15, 2026
Audi Q8 e-tron Variants Dimensions Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Tranportation Capabilities Technology Multimedia Reviews Options Guide

The programmable garage-door opener supports up to eight doors. A stored door can be associated with a GPS location so that the car suggests the relevant control when it approaches. The garage-door system must be HomeLink compatible.
