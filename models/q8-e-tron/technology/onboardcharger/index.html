Audi Q8 e-tron onboard Charger

Audi Q8 e-tron has an onboard charger for level 1 and level 2 charging.

Last updated Dec 20, 2025
Audi Q8 e-tron Variants Dimensions Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Tranportation Capabilities Technology Multimedia Reviews Options Guide

The onboard charger is responsible for converting AC to DC.

The standard onboard charger support up to 11KW AC charging

In the US the charging port has a J1772 connector to connect to the car, while in Europe it has a Type 2 connector.

To charge the car from AC you need a Wallbox to connect to or the charging system that can connect to the domestic outlet.

Optional charge port

You can order an additional chargeport on the passenger side. This is option ID JS1

Optional 22KW charger

You can order an additional charging unit to the car (AX5). This gives the car 22KW charging capability on 400Volt 32A 3 phases connection.

You need to order the additional port JS1 and the Audi Charging System Connect NW2
