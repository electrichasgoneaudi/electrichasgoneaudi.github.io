Audi Q4 e-tron safety technology

The Audi Q4 e-tron has several safety technologies designed to reduce injuries in a collision.

Airbags

Audi Q4 e-tron has several airbags as standard.

Front airbags

Head airbags

Both front and rear are protect with head airbags.

Side Airbags front

In front there is standard with side Airbags build in to the seat that protects the body.

Center Airbags front

In front there is standard with airbags between driver and passenger to protect them hitting each other during a side collision.

Side Airbags rear

In rear the side air bags is optional. The option ID is 6C4

Seat belts

Front seats belts have reversible belt tensioners at the front
