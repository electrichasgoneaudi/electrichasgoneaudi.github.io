Audi Q4 e-tron driver assistance systems

Audi Q4 e-tron has several driving assistance systems to increase safety and make every drive more comfortable.

Last updated Dec 20, 2025
Audi Q4 e-tron Variants Dimensions Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Tranportation Capabilities Technology Multimedia Reviews Knowledge exchange Options Guide

One front radar, one front camera, four surround-view cameras, two rear radars, and eight ultrasonic sensors: When fully equipped, the sensor system of the Audi Q4 e-tron and the Q4 Sportback e-tron covers a large sensing field and a large number of traffic situations.

Important driver assist systems come as standard, the optional systems are subdivided into four packages: the assist package (PYK), the assist package plus (PYL), the assist package pro (PY7), and the safety package (PYU).

Below you find a detailed explanation how each driver assistance functionality works.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron 360 degree cameras

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be delivered with 360 degree camera. The four 360 degree cameras on the vehicle scan the immediate vicinity and display obstacles within. Drivers can choose from different views that simplify parking and maneuvering.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron active lane assist

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with Active lane assist. Operating at speeds from 65 km/h (40.4 mph), Audi active lane assist helps the driver keep the vehicle in the driving lane.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron adaptive cruise assist

    You can order the Audi Q4 e-tron with adaptive cruise assist. The adaptive cruise assist combines adaptive cruise control and active lane assist.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron adaptive cruise control with speed limiter.

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be delivered with adaptive cruise control. The Adaptive cruise control (ACC) supports the driver within the system limits

  • Audi Q4 e-tron collision avoidance assist

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with collision avoidance assist. Collision avoidance assist helps the driver steer around an obstacle in a critical situation.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron cross traffic assist rear

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be equipped with cross traffic assist rear. Cross traffic assist rear warns the driver of approaching vehicles it deems critical when slowly backing up, such as when pulling out of a perpendicular parking spot.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron cruise control

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be delivered with cruise control. Cruise control maintains a desired driving speed constantly starting at around 30 km/h (18.6 mph), provided that it can be maintained by engine power and engine braking effects.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron distance warning

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with distance warning. The distance warning warns you when you are driving to close to the car in front of you.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron predictive efficiency assist

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with predictive efficiency assist. The efficiency assist can help the driver to anticipate the driving route and adopt an economical driving style.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron emergency assist

    Audi Q4 can be delivered with emergency assist. Emergency assist detects, within system limits, when the driver is inactive. In such a case, the system assumes control of the vehicle and automatically brakes it to a standstill in its own lane.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron exit warning

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be equipped with exit warning. Exit warning improves safety in city traffic. If the car has come to a stop and other vehicles or bicyclists classified as critical are approaching from the rear, the system warns passengers not to open the doors.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron hold assist

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with hold assist. Hold assist enables convenient drive-offs when the car is on the usual inclines and descents of street traffic, and it prevents the vehicle from rolling.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron parking system plus

    You can get Audi Q4 e-tron delivered with parking system plus. Parking system plus informs the driver, visually and audibly, about obstacles in front of and behind the vehicle.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron parking system rear

    Parking system rear is standard on Audi Q4 e-tron. Parking system rears informs the driver, visually and audibly, about obstacles behind the vehicle.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron pre sense basic

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be equipped with Audi pre sense basic assist system. Audi Pre sense basic initiates preventive safety measures for occupants as soon as it recognizes a critical driving state.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron pre sense front

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped wioth pre sense front. Pre sense front utilize the data of radar sensors and the front camera to compute the probability of a collision. Within the limitations of the system, it warns of collision threats and initiates braking at specific vehicle speeds.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron pre sense rear

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be equipped with Audi pre sense rear. Audi pre sense rear uses radar sensors in the rear bumper to detect an impending rear-end collision, and it initiates preventive safety measures.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron reversing camera

    Audi Q4 e-tron ca be delivered with reversing camera. The reversing camera simplifies maneuvering by showing the zone behind the vehicle on the MMI display.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron side assist

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be equipped with side side assist. The Audi side assist lane-changing assistant helps the driver to change lanes at driving speeds of 15 km/h (9.3 mph) and faster.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron speed limiter

    Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with a speed limiter. The configurable speed limiter limits driving speed to a value set by the driver within the speed range of 30 to 250 km/h (18.6 to 155.3 mph) – which is very helpful within city limits or construction zones, for example.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron traffic sign recognition

    Audi Q4 e-tron can be equipped with traffic sign recognition. Camera-based traffic sign recognition detects traffic signs such as speed limit signs (including digital signs), no passing zones, entry restriction signs and other auxiliary signs, and it shows them to the driver in graphic form.

  • Audi Q4 e-tron turn assist

    The Audi Q4 e-tron is equipped with turn assist. Turn assist monitors the road lane with oncoming traffic by means of radar sensors, the front camera and, in certain models, a laser scanner.
