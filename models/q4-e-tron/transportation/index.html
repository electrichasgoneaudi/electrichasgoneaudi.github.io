Audi Q4 e-tron

Transportation capabilities

In addition to the 5 seats, the Q4 e-tron has quite large trunk in addition to roof storage and trailer capabilities.

Last updated Jul 15, 2026

Trunk

The cargo volume on Q4 e-tron is on 520 liter. When rear seats are down the total storage capacity is 1490 liter.

See below for Banana box test for Audi Q4 e-tron.

Roof storage

You can store up to 75kg on the roof. On the SUV version you have rails so you can easily mount a roof box.

Towing capacity

Audi Q4 e-tron can drag trailers up to 1200kg with Audi Q4 e-tron 45/50 Quattro and 1000kg Audi Q4 e-tron 40. The towing hitch has option id 1M6. An optional towing hitch preparation has option id 1D7

Trailer consumption

The above trailer gave an consumption on 32kWh/100km.
