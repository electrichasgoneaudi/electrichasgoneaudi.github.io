Audi Q5 e-tron

Audi Q5 e-tron technology

The Audi Q5 e-tron features a comprehensive suite of driver assistance systems, a multi-sensor setup, and China-specific connectivity features.

Last updated May 9, 2026
Audi Q5 e-tron Variants Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Technology

The Audi Q5 e-tron combines Audi’s driver assistance technology with the MEB platform’s proven safety and connectivity systems, tailored for the Chinese market.

Driver assistance systems

The Q5 e-tron is equipped with a comprehensive suite of driver assistance features:

  • Adaptive cruise control
  • Lane keeping assist and lane departure warning
  • Forward collision warning with automatic emergency braking
  • Rear cross traffic alert
  • Blind spot monitoring
  • Reversing camera
  • Parking sensors (front and rear)
  • Hill start assist
  • Traffic sign recognition

Sensors

The Q5 e-tron uses a multi-sensor approach for driver assistance:

  • Ultrasonic sensors — covering all corners for close-range parking assistance
  • Cameras — front, rear, and corner-mounted cameras for surround awareness
  • Radar — forward-facing radar for adaptive cruise control and emergency braking

Sound system

The Q5 e-tron is available with an upgraded audio system.

Connectivity

The Q5 e-tron is equipped with Audi connect services and a China-specific infotainment ecosystem, including voice control and over-the-air update capability.
