Audi Q5 e-tron variants

The Audi Q5 e-tron is available in two variants — Q5 40 e-tron with rear-wheel drive and Q5 50 e-tron quattro with all-wheel drive.

Last updated May 9, 2026
Audi Q5 e-tron Variants Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Technology

The Audi Q5 e-tron is offered in two variants, both using the same 83.4 kWh battery but differing in power output and drivetrain.

Audi Q5 e-tron 40

The entry variant with rear-wheel drive and a single electric motor.
