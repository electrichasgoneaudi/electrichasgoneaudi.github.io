Audi Q5 e-tron

The Audi Q5 e-tron is a China-exclusive fully electric SUV built on the MEB platform, offered in two variants with up to 560 km CLTC range.

The Audi Q5 e-tron is a China-exclusive, fully electric SUV built on the MEB platform, shared with the Audi Q4 e-tron, Skoda Enyaq, and Volkswagen ID.4. Positioned above the Q4 e-tron in Audi’s China lineup, it offers a longer wheelbase, more passenger space, and a choice of rear-wheel or all-wheel drive.

The Q5 e-tron had its world premiere on 18 November 2021 and is produced and sold exclusively in China.

Variants

Two variants are available:

Design and dimensions

The Q5 e-tron follows Audi’s current design language with a clean, progressive SUV profile. The extended wheelbase gives it a commanding presence on the road.

Key dimensions:

  • Length: 4,876 mm
  • Width: 1,860 mm (excluding mirrors)
  • Height: 1,675 mm
  • Wheelbase: 2,965 mm

Interior

The Q5 e-tron offers a five-seat configuration with a focus on space and digital connectivity. The cabin features a dual-screen setup with a virtual cockpit and MMI touchscreen, along with optional seat ventilation.

→ Full interior details: seats, screens and features

Drivetrain

Both variants use the same 83.4 kWh NMC battery pack, supported by the MEB platform’s proven electric architecture. The suspension combines a MacPherson strut front with a multi-link rear, and optional adaptive damping is available.

The charge port is located on the left rear side of the vehicle.
