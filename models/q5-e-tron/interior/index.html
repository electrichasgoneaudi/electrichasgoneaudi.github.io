Audi Q5 e-tron interior

The Audi Q5 e-tron offers a five-seat premium interior with a dual-screen setup, optional seat ventilation, and a spacious cabin enabled by the long MEB wheelbase.

Last updated May 9, 2026
Audi Q5 e-tron Variants Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Technology

The Audi Q5 e-tron cabin is designed around comfort and digital connectivity, with the extended 2,965 mm wheelbase providing generous space for all occupants.

Seating

The Q5 e-tron seats five across two rows.

Front seats

  • Driver and passenger seat configuration
  • Height-adjustable headrests
  • Optional seat ventilation

Rear seats

The rear bench accommodates three passengers with generous legroom provided by the long wheelbase.

Screens and user interface

The Q5 e-tron features Audi’s digital cabin setup:

  • Virtual cockpit digital instrument cluster
  • MMI touch infotainment screen
  • Voice control

Storage and cargo

  • Trunk capacity: 560 litres
  • No front trunk (frunk)
  • Max roof cargo: 75 kg
