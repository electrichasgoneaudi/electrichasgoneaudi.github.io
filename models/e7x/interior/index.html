AUDI E7X

AUDI E7X interior

The AUDI E7X offers a five-seat premium interior with multiple large screens, voice control, and optional features such as seat ventilation, massage, and a head-up display.

Last updated May 9, 2026
AUDI E7X Variants Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Technology Multimedia

The AUDI E7X interior is designed around the tech-savvy Chinese customer, combining premium materials and craftsmanship with a deeply digital and connected cabin experience.

Seating configuration

The E7X is configured as a five-seat SUV with two front seats and a three-seat rear bench.

Front seats

The front seats offer a driver and passenger seat configuration with:

  • Height-adjustable headrests
  • Optional adjustable thigh support
  • Optional seat ventilation
  • Optional massage function

Rear seats

The rear features a three-seat bench providing generous space thanks to the 3,060 mm wheelbase.

Interior colors and inlays

Interior color choices vary by variant:

Pioneer and Pioneer Pro offer two colors, both included:

  • Phantom Gray (幻影灰) — deep shadow tones, timeless character
  • Quantum Silver (量子银)

Pioneer Smart Long-Range and Pioneer quattro expand the choice to three colors:

  • Flowing Gold Brown (鎏金棕) — standard
  • Quantum Silver (量子银) — included
  • Ink Blue (墨韵蓝) — ¥4,000

Flagship quattro offers two colors, both included at no extra cost:

  • Quantum Silver (量子银) — standard
  • Ink Blue (墨韵蓝) — included (free, vs ¥4,000 on lower variants)

Two inlay options are available across all variants, both included. Default inlay differs by variant:

  • Openpore North American Natural Chestnut — standard on Pioneer through Pioneer quattro
  • Openpore North American Natural Oak — standard on Flagship quattro

Headliner

The E7X comes with the Articite premium suede headliner as standard — an OEKO-TEX certified, baby-skin-grade material covering the full roof panel, ABC pillars, sunvisors and trim frames (value ¥3,000).

Audi Haute Couture Luxury Package (Pioneer Smart Long-Range and above)

The Audi Haute Couture Luxury Package (奥迪高定奢享套装, value ¥14,000) is core standard equipment on the Pioneer Smart Long-Range and represents the highest material specification in the E7X lineup:

  • Articite premium suede headliner — OEKO-TEX certified, covers full roof, ABC pillars, sunvisors and trim
  • Feinnappa full-grain calf leather (Feinnappa 全粒面小牛皮) — premium full-grain leather upholstery throughout the cabin

Audi Smart Living Room (Pioneer Pro through Pioneer quattro)

From the Pioneer Pro upwards, the Audi Smart Living Room package transforms the rear cabin experience:

  • 21.4-inch QD Mini LED rear ceiling screen — Apple XDR-grade, supports wired/wireless projection from Switch, PS5 and more
  • Smart four-season cooler/warmer box — integrated climate-controlled storage
  • Zero-gravity front passenger seat — fully reclinable comfort position
  • Cloud comfort aviation-style rear seats

Palace-Class Four-Seat First-Class Cabin (Flagship quattro)

The Flagship quattro replaces the Smart Living Room with the Palace-Class Zero-Pressure Four-Seat First-Class Cabin (殿堂级零压四座头等舱, value ¥59,000) — a significantly elevated rear experience:

  • 21.4-inch QD Mini LED rear ceiling screen — star screen, smart four-season cooler/warmer box
  • Dual rear zero-gravity seats — both rear outboard seats recline to 120° (near-flat), vs single front passenger zero-G on lower variants
  • 23-point pneumatic adjustment with airbag-based support shaping (per seat)
  • 16-point massage function
  • Electric extendable footrest with up to 400 mm of travel

Health certification

The entire cabin carries OEKO-TEX® mother & baby grade health certification, covering seats, seatbelts, steering wheel, dashboard, trim panels, cupholders and more — all verified against over 1,000 harmful substances (value ¥8,000).

Screens and user interface

The AUDI E7X features a full-width multi-screen cabin architecture:

  • 27-inch 4K panoramic dashboard display — a single curved screen spanning the full instrument and infotainment width
  • 21.4-inch QD Mini LED rear ceiling screen — deploys from the headliner, Apple XDR-grade, supports wired/wireless projection from Switch, PS5 and other devices
  • Rear center console touchscreen
  • Optional head-up display
  • Audi Smart Island — smart interaction hub

AUDI Assistant 2.0 — the voice and AI interface, co-developed with ByteDance. Delivers significantly enhanced conversational AI capability and deep integration with Chinese digital services, apps and payment platforms. Standard on all variants.

The cabin does not support Android Auto, reflecting AUDI’s focus on its own deeply integrated China-specific software ecosystem.
