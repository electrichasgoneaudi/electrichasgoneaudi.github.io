AUDI E7X

AUDI E7X exterior

The AUDI E7X features a bold, forward-looking SUV design with six available paint colors, vertical Matrix LED headlights, and a distinctive monolithic body language.

Last updated Jul 15, 2026
AUDI E7X Variants Exterior Specifications Interior Drivetrain Technology Multimedia

The AUDI E7X exterior design is directly inspired by the AUDI E SUV concept, translating a progressive and confident design language into a production-ready large electric SUV.

Design language

Key design characteristics of the E7X exterior include:

  • AUDI Light Frame — a wraparound front architecture with over 1,000 individually controllable triangular LED elements, creating a distinctive light identity at the front and sides
  • Vertically arranged digital Matrix LED headlights with lane and position marking capability
  • Black wraparound loop integrating lights and sensors
  • Frameless doors for a clean, uninterrupted body surface
  • Short overhangs and powerfully sculpted wheel arches
  • SUV body style with a commanding road presence

The AUDI brand identity — with the brand name written in capital letters rather than the four-ring Audi logo — is prominently featured, underlining the distinct positioning of AUDI as a China-specific brand.

Dimensions

The E7X occupies a commanding footprint in the large premium SUV segment:

  • Length: 5,049 mm
  • Width: 1,997 mm (excluding mirrors)
  • Height: 1,710 mm
  • Wheelbase: 3,060 mm
  • Turning circle: 10.3 m

Paint colors

The AUDI E7X is available in six exterior paint colors:

  • Monday Black (solid black)
  • Flowing Silver (metallic silver)
  • Nebula Purple (metallic purple)
  • Dark Rock Beige (light beige)
  • Misty Blue (metallic blue)
  • Titanium Gray (metallic gray)

Headlights

The E7X features vertically arranged digital Matrix LED headlights as standard. From the Pioneer Smart Long-Range upwards, the headlights are upgraded to the Star Diamond Light Blade Upgrade Kit (星钻光幕光刀升级套装, value ¥22,000) — incorporating DLP (Digital Light Projection) high-definition digital projection headlights, 8 programmable welcome and farewell light animations, and a linked lighting design across the full front light signature. On Pioneer Smart Long-Range and Pioneer quattro this kit is a limited-time gift; on the Flagship quattro it is core standard equipment.

Wheels

  • Pioneer: 21-inch 8-spoke alloy wheels (silver finish, CLTC 705 km)
  • Pioneer Pro: 21-inch 8-spoke black alloy wheels (CLTC 691 km)
  • Pioneer Smart Long-Range: 21-inch 8-spoke black alloy (included, CLTC 751 km) or 22-inch 8-spoke high-spec alloy with Brembo sport calipers and Audi floating hub covers (¥5,500 extra, CLTC 730 km)
  • Pioneer quattro: 21-inch 8-spoke black alloy (included, CLTC 635 km) or 22-inch 8-spoke black alloy with Brembo sport calipers and Audi floating hub covers (¥5,500 extra, CLTC 615 km)

Mirrors

From the Pioneer Pro upwards, the E7X comes standard with digital camera-based side mirrors replacing conventional glass mirrors. Physical mirrors are available as a no-cost option for those who prefer them.

Panoramic roof

The Smart Electrochromic Panoramic Roof features a 2.195 m² glass area with electronically adjustable tinting, blocking 99.99% of UV, 99.9% of infrared and 99.85% of solar radiation.
