Audi E5 Sportback interior

The Audi E5 Sportback interior is a digital-first space with a full-width panoramic display, multicolor ambient lighting, standard seat ventilation, and generous rear-seat space.

The Audi E5 Sportback interior is conceived as a digital-first living space, combining a clean horizontal dashboard layout with premium materials, a full-width display architecture, and deep connectivity. The design reflects Audi’s shift toward software-defined vehicles for the Chinese market.

Screens and user interface

The E5 Sportback features a full-width panoramic dual-screen dashboard spanning the driver display and infotainment areas in a unified layout. There is no physical divider between the two screens — the system presents as a single continuous display surface.

  • Digital instrument cluster — high-resolution, customizable views
  • Central infotainment touchscreen — native AUDI OS with China-specific apps and services
  • Head-up display — standard on Pioneer Plus, Pioneer quattro, and quattro; not available on base Pioneer
  • Voice control — standard on all variants
  • Apple CarPlay — supported
  • Android Auto not available

The infotainment system is built on a proprietary AUDI OS platform co-developed with SAIC, with a native third-party app store, built-in navigation, real-time traffic, and local payment integration.

Seating configuration

The E5 Sportback is a five-seat Sportback with two front seats and a three-seat rear bench. The flat floor enhances rear footroom and makes the center rear position more comfortable.

Front seats

  • Electrically adjustable (fore/aft, recline, height, cushion angle)
  • Heated (standard)
  • Ventilated (standard)
  • Driver seat memory
  • Lumbar support
  • Optional massage function

Rear seats

  • 60:40 split-folding bench
  • Manual height-adjustable headrests
  • ISOFIX on outer rear positions
  • Optional heated outer rear seats
  • Rear seat trunk access: 1,233 L with seats folded (from 347 L standard)

Steering wheel

The E5 Sportback features a flat-bottom and flat-top circular steering wheel as standard:

  • Heated (standard)
  • Auto-retract easy-entry function
  • Physical buttons and scroll wheels for media, cruise control, and voice
  • Manually adjustable (tilt and telescoping)

Ambient lighting

Multicolor ambient lighting is standard across all variants, with coverage across:

  • Dashboard
  • Door panels
  • Footwells

Colors and themes are customizable via the infotainment system.

Climate control

  • 2-zone automatic climate control (driver and passenger independently)
  • Heat pump — standard across all variants for efficient heating in cold conditions
  • Rear tinted windows from B-pillar rearward (standard)
